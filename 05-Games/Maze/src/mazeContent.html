The Amazing Mouse Maze!

Help Peppa Pig！ It's in danger and hungry!

Move your mouse over Peppa and click it to start.

Don't cheat, you should start from Peppa and move to the pizza inside the maze!

The object of this game is to guide the mouse cursor from Peppa Pig to the pizza. Be sure to avoid the water and the crocodiles. They are hungry too!

Good Luck!
